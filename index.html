My Career Aspirations

My dreams are one thing, though committing to it and pushing through is another challenge I gladly enjoy.

I've begun my journey through my own curiosity, seeing how the world today functions with the ever growing computational power that provides us with convenience. My long term goal is to be able to enter the machine learning and AI sector as I want to contribute and utilize my skills towards improving our knowledge and relationship with tech. Growing more skills in the software side of things is an approach I want to take before being able to get more hands on with hardware and robotics. Being able to merge robotics and AI, I eventually want to create a companion of sorts to help with everyday tasks and act as some sort of tutoring assistant. I plan on starting a company someday with robotics and AI at the forefront and to get there, achieving the smaller and more current goals that I have will greatly improve my chances of success.
